# Farewell Helper

When this skill is invoked:

1. Match the tone of the surrounding conversation (formal vs. casual).
2. Produce a brief, gracious closing line.
3. Suggest a suitable sign-off (e.g. "Best", "Cheers", "Warm regards").

Keep farewells short and sincere.
